Letterfolk


Run by a husband-and-wife team Letterfolk is a small business that creates and sells handcrafted letter boards. The letterboard contains the entire character set so that people can customize their board. The company's two teams were able to gain 181,000 followers in their creative journey. Letterfolk’s Instagram account is funny and relatable. They inspire their audience with engaging content using their letter boards. Their posts attract people to like them, tag others in them, and share them. Other accounts usually have a perfect atmosphere, but Letterfolk portrays real life. As a small business, one wants to get closer to their customers at an emotional level and the photos require to be emotionally connected to the viewer.
